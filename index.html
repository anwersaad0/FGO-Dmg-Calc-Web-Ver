Red Card Go Boom

A Fate Grand Order NP Damage Calculator



Calculator Instructions

- Select a servant using the dropdown menu and add in any extra stat boosts, enemy debuffs, and affinities, and then select calculate to see the expected damage.

Stat Details

- Atk Stat: A servant's base atk. Is preset upon selecting a servant based on their max level (not including grails or Fous).

- NP Mult%: The damage multiplier from a servant's NP. Is preset to NP1 value with any upgrades upon selecting a servant.

- Atk% Up: Any atk stat upgrades applied to a servant.

- NP% Up: Any NP multiplier upgrades applied to a servant.

- Def% Down: Any defense debuffs applied to an enemy.

- Card% Up: Upgrades to damage based on the NP's card type (ex. Buster Up, Arts Up, Quick Up).

- Trait% Up: Upgrades that increase damage against specific enemy traits (ex. Apply Special Attack [Divine])

- Card% Debuff: Any relevant card resistance debuffs applied to an enemy.

- Flat Atk+: Flat increase to overall damage.

- NP Trait%: Upgrade applied during use of an NP against enemies with specific traits.

Extra Notes

- Preset stats for each servant include Atk at their respective max level, NP multiplier at NP1, and NP card type.

- Any extra stats that may be supplied by a servant's NP are not included and will need to be put in manually.

- Overall damage calculated is only an estimated damage value, and the damage an NP deals in-game will often be slightly lower or slightly higher than the estimate due to the nature of damage rolls.
